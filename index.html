PROVISIONS
A warrior is only as strong as their fuel. Manage your resources wisely.
2,500 cal

Daily Calories

Target 2,400–2,600 cal/day to lose 1–1.5 lbs/week. Adjust down slightly if weight loss stalls for 2+ weeks.

190 g

Daily Protein

180–200g protein per day is non-negotiable. This preserves muscle mass as you lose fat. Hit this number every single day.

🍗 Best Protein Sources

Chicken breast, 93% ground beef, eggs & egg whites, Greek yogurt, cottage cheese, protein shakes (whey or casein), canned tuna/salmon, turkey breast.

🍠 Carb Timing

Eat most of your carbs before and after training. Oats, white rice, sweet potato, fruit, and bread are all fine. Timing > source.

💧 Hydration

At least 100 oz of water per day. Your hunger is often thirst. Drink a full glass before each meal. No sugary drinks or juice.

🚫 Cut First

Alcohol, soda, juice, energy drinks, fast food more than once a week. These are the biggest hidden calorie traps. Cut them and watch the scale move.

📋 Sample Daily Meal Plan

Breakfast (7am): 4 scrambled eggs + 1 cup oats + black coffee — ~600 cal, 40g protein

Lunch (12pm): 8oz chicken breast + 1 cup rice + vegetables — ~550 cal, 60g protein

Pre-Workout Snack (3pm): Greek yogurt + banana — ~250 cal, 20g protein

Dinner (7pm): 8oz 93% ground beef + sweet potato + salad — ~650 cal, 55g protein

Evening (9pm): Cottage cheese or casein shake — ~200 cal, 25g protein
